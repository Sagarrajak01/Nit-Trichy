About Us

Our Legacy

Established in 1964 as the Regional Engineering College (REC) Tiruchirappalli, a joint venture between the Government of India and the Government of Tamil Nadu, the National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli (NIT Trichy) has emerged as one of India’s foremost technical institutions. Granted Deemed University status in 2003 and recognized as an Institute of National Importance under the National Institutes of Technology Act, 2007, NIT Trichy has consistently set benchmarks in engineering, science, technology, and management education

Reaserch and Innovation

NIT Trichy is a hub for innovation, housing state-of-the-art research facilities and the Centre for Entrepreneurial Development and Incubation (CEDI). CEDI supports technology startups through initiatives like the Technological Incubation and Development of Entrepreneurs (TIDE) program, funded by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology. Our modern central library, with over 2.5 lakh documents and 5,000 e-journals, supports cutting-edge research and learning.

Industry and Alumini connect

NIT Trichy is celebrated for its strong industry linkages, earning the title of "Best Industry-Linked NIT" by the Confederation of Indian Industry in 2015. Our alumni, including luminaries like Natarajan Chandrasekaran (Chairman, Tata Sons) and Rajesh Gopinathan (former CEO, Tata Consultancy Services), lead major global corporations, reflecting our commitment to nurturing transformative leaders.

Social Responsibility

We take pride in our contributions to society, recognized by national awards from FICCI for social responsibility and ASSOCHAM for leveraging ICT tools in education. Initiatives like the Accessible India Campaign and anti-ragging measures underscore our commitment to inclusivity and ethical practices.

Why Nit Trichy

With a legacy of over six decades, NIT Trichy combines academic rigor, innovative research, and holistic development to shape future-ready professionals. Whether you’re a student, researcher, or industry partner, NIT Trichy offers a dynamic environment to learn, innovate, and lead. Join us in our journey to create a better tomorrow through technology and knowledge.
